PLAYTESTING FEEDBACK:
weapon ideas:
-Basic turret
-Slower stronger turret, less range
-Frag bomb
-Unlimited range missle (slow but one shots basic enemy) 
-“Napalm clouder” leaves a chip damage field, not across full x/y but maybe a set volume that increases with upgrade (but never full x/y).
-Orbital mine launcher (launches magnetic mine exact mechanics tbd)
-Repulsor beam (makes an enemy retreat for a set amount of time)
-Aerial hack array (“converts” enemy to become kamikaze unit that attacks other invaders, closest unit for simplicity or self destruct if alone, damage scaling tbd)
-Blockade launcher (purely defensive unit, slowly builds and launches physical barriers, not very high up but can soak 2-3 basic enemy impacts.  might be tricky to balance)
-Drone launcher (machine guns can’t reach spawn heights, missle can but very slow, drones can reach anywhere but less damage than basic gun, however can swarm and if they concentrate on one unit can equal the basic gun or surpass (maximum of 2x maybe, needs to be balanced out).  Unsure if destroyed by enemy or have limited life based on like (only lives x amount of shots, would need to be balanced with spawn rate but this isn’t the final design decision)
-Nuke: not automatic, can wipe all but bosses however wipes player towers as well except basic missle defense unit.
-Missle defense unit: one is given freely as missle defence waves are integral gameplay.  Can be upgrade for projectile speed and missle spread, multiple can be had for increased firing rate. (Cost vs efficiency at defense to be balanced)

design notes: 
-enemy spawning is ok for the first few levels to come in big batches as it is, but as levels go on, it should become more irregular and “random attack” feeling, not reduction in amount but spread delivery, less tightly grouped together.
-economy seems too generous at present but that can be tweaked after other buildout
-for missle launch phases, perhaps a small overlay “radar hud” showing the main enemies as simple dots and their distance from land, a simple xy plot type thing, so a player can peek out if the graph shows something urgent. actually with our semi-fixed camera pitch, this graph might be handy to persist at all times, since z/height distance isn’t immediately readable.
-volume of stronger enemies is too low as stages progress, large swarms of the basic unit are still too easy to take down.  not sure if weapon or enemy strength needs tuning, or if this is an economy lever (too much money not enough challenge etc).  will need deliberation.  
-quick start bonus is unnecessary.
